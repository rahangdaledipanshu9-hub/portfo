AIR-1 National Grand Winner

N-PHANTOM

Multifunctional Stealth Coating for Defence

A low-cost multi-signature coating system engineered to simultaneously reduce Radar Cross-Section (RCS), IR emissions, and thermal signature — achieving three-band stealth in a single retrofittable layer. Built using thermally stable acrylic/siloxane binders with IR-reflective pigments and iron-oxide ferrites, all from India's domestic supply chain.

BaFe₁₂O₁₉ Synthesis

Barium Hexaferrite for EMI & RAM Applications

Synthesised hexagonal crystalline barium hexaferrite via sol-gel and combustion routes using citric acid and sugar fuels, with calcination at 700–900 °C to achieve high-purity microparticles with hard-magnetic and microwave-absorbing properties.
